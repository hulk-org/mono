**Why CAPTURE > write-down:**

"Write-down" is passive — recording a fact that's already settled. "Capture" is active — TRANSITIONING something from un-typed wild state to typed-substrate-record state. Per `everything-is-quantum-state-machine` axiom: capture IS the state-machine transition verb.

The workflow moment is fleeting — context dissolves, attention drifts. wd CATCHES it before it escapes. The `context-limit-approaching` event triggering automatic wd is the substrate equivalent of "throw the ball before the Pokémon escapes into the tall grass."
