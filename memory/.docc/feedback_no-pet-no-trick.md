The pet-and-trick vocabulary is fully retired. Komo are not pets, they don't perform tricks. Use **patrol** for the macro-verb (journey-shape: a digikoma riding a chartered route on recurrence) and **scan** for the micro-verb (per-station inspection performed while on patrol). Use **action** as the schema-field name (per the `koma-org` `spec.json` convention and `KomaAction` in core-entity-set v0.9.0 organism-schemas v0.4.0). Never use **trick**.

**Why:** 2026-04-12 the operator killed "Pet" because "you can't call Apple Intelligence a pet" — recorded in `insights/naming-evolution-pet-to-komo-2026-04-12.md`, which retired trick → action and pet → Komo. On 2026-04-29 the operator reinforced both rules and specified the precise verbs in three turns: *"what is a trick? noooo... it's a scan,"* then *"trick is the old pet analogy - we have to move forward from that and rewrite everything about that,"* then *"they scan / patrol right?"* — the last clarifying that scan and patrol are two scales of the same architecture, not synonyms. "Scan" carries the inspection-without-mutation semantics; "patrol" carries the recurrence + vigilance + chartered-route semantics. Pet/trick obscured both. The 2026-04-29 conversation also locked the surrounding architecture: routine charters traversal → factory mints digikoma → conveyor boards them onto a train → digikoma patrols the chartered route → at each station, performs a scan → debark with bookend receipts → host applies imprint conditional on both stamps.

**How to apply:** Two scales:

- **Patrol** (macro) — the recurring chartered journey. A patrol exists when a routine is in charge of recurrence; routine fires → patrol begins → train rolls → digikoma rides → debark + receipt → next routine fire restarts the patrol. A one-shot manual run is a *tour*, not a patrol.
- **Scan** (micro) — the per-station inspection. What the digikoma does at each stop on its patrol. A scan is bounded, mutation-free in the field-world, and its findings live in the offboarding receipt until the host applies the imprint.

Use **action** for the schema-slot/catalog noun. Use **anatomy** for traversal presets (formerly "species"). Phrases banned: "the unit's tricks," "trick selection," "trick binding," "trick catalog," "trick-budget," "pet trainer," "give it the X trick," "training the pet," "tricks the Komo can do." If a saved doctrine memory still uses "trick" or "pet," update it on contact rather than perpetuating the dead vocabulary. The naming-evolution insight is the historical record of why the words died and should NOT be rewritten — it's evidence, not active doctrine.
